Private Equity Investment

Rongowhakaata Iwi Asset Holding company Private Equity Investment Strategy December 2017

Introduction

The purpose of this document is to describe RIAHC private equity strategy.

Our objectives

RIAHC is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Rongowhakaata Iwi Trust. The core purpose of the Asset Holding company is to manage the assets of the Rongowhakaata group in a way that:

  • Generates a sufficient financial return to fund Iwi activities;
  • Safeguards and grows the value of the putea for future generations; and
  • Contributes to the Trust’s broader purposes/kaupapa in a meaningful way.

Our parameters for private equity investments

As part of our wider investment strategy the Investment Company is actively seeking private equity investment opportunities that meet the following criteria:

  • Provides a minimum risk-adjusted return of 5%.
  • The total entity value is between $XXm - $XXm.
  • A minimum shareholding of 25%.
  • Funds being raised for expansion capital with strong stable cash flow.
  • The presence of compatible high-value owners
  • There is an investment time horizon of 5-10 years
  • Ascertainable exit opportunities
  • There is the ability to continue to add value
  • The opportunity meets the Investment Company’s portfolio diversification requirements
  • Sectors of particular initial interest to the Investment Company are; Agriculture, Health, Education, Aquaculture and Technology.

Other factors we will consider

When reviewing private equity opportunities, the Investment Company will also give consideration to:

  • The ability for the Investment Company to contribute to governance capability and disciplines to the target entity.
  • The ability for the Investment Company to work with like-minded and/or more experienced investors.
  • Whether there is the ability to strengthen the targets’ cash-flow, undertake effective change management, create shared value and take advantage of any lateral opportunities.
  • Whether there are opportunities to create pathways for Iwi members into high value sectors.
  • Whether it provides an opportunity for investment into culturally resonant sectors/activities.
