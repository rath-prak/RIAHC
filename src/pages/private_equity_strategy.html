Private Equity Investment

Our objectives

RIAHC is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Rongowhakaata Iwi Trust. The core purpose of the Asset Holding company is to manage the assets of the Rongowhakaata group in a way that:

  • Generates a sufficient financial return to fund Iwi activities;
  • Safeguards and grows the value of the putea for future generations; and
  • Contributes to the Trust’s broader purposes/kaupapa in a meaningful way.

Our parameters for private equity investments

We are actively seeking private equity investment opportunities that meet the minimum criteria:

  • Provides a minimum risk-adjusted return of 5%.
  • A minimum shareholding of 25%.
  • Ability to continue to add value.
  • The opportunity meets the Investment Company’s portfolio diversification requirements.

Other factors we will consider

  • Create pathways for Iwi members into high value sectors.
  • Work with like-minded and/or more experienced investors.
  • Create any lateral or horizontal opportunities.
  • Investment into culturally resonant sectors/activities.
